Golden Cupcake

The Golden Cupcake is a health-boosted twist on a traditional dessert. Packed with wholesome ingredients, it delivers rich flavor and vital nutrients without compromising on taste. Its golden hue comes naturally from turmeric and golden sweet potatoes, offering anti-inflammatory benefits and a vibrant appearance.

  • Calories: 180 - 220 kcal
  • Protein: 6 - 8g
  • Fiber 3 - 4g
  • Healthy Fats: 10 - 12g
  • Sugar (natural): 6 - 8g
  • Vitamins: A, E, B12
  • Minerals: Iron, Calcium, Magnesium
